Sedigheh Moradi (Sophie)

About me:

I am a PhD candidate in the Linguistics Department at Stony Brook University, working on my dissertation, co-advised by Mark Aronoff and Thomas Graf. My current research deals with monotonic functions (mappings) in morphological paradigms.

Interests:

  • Inflectional Paradigms
  • Morphological Complexity
  • Typological morphosyntax
  • Typology of Iranian Languages
  • Natural Language Understanding
